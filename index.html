--- layout: page # Index page --- Hello! My name is Federico and I am an Italian Senior Machine Learning Engineer working across Machine Learning, Computer Vision and Robotics.
As a kid I was alternating between unscrewing everything that had electronics in it and building anything that could move with Legos.

Then one day, my dad taught me how to use a battery to power up small bulbs and motors, and in that moment the engineer in me was born.
My passion for robotics came shortly after when I saw Cybot on TV during an ad for Real Robots magazine. Unfortunately, my parents never allowed me to purchase a single number of it. But this pushed me to hand-solder my first PIC programmer to build a very simple robot based on micro switches.
From there, one thing led to another and this has become my career. I have changed a lot, but the curiosity in me never did and it still fuels me to learn something new everyday.

When I'm not in front of a computer screen, I might be in front of a microphone instead, singing and playing my guitar.
I also enjoy spending time outdoors in nature, for example hiking in the Italian Dolomites or Colli Euganei. But I like spending my fair share of time chilling on the beach while the sun sets as well.
